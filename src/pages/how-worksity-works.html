How Worksity Works

We connect local talents to local people and local businesses to build towards a profitable community where each member’s wellbeing and economy is enhanced through our objective.

Connect: Building direct connections will support and enrich you every day. By delegating your tasks to a trusty face in your community, you are contributing to the development of that community, the economy, and the wellbeing of others.

Be Active (Physically, mentally and spiritually): Offer your competence to the local community or give others the opportunity to offer theirs. Go out and do it together.

Take notice (Live consciously): If you notice there is a chance or possibility for enhancement, do it. If there is a way you can help others, offer it. Be aware of the world around you and how you’re feeling. If it is great, share it. If it can be improved, help to change it.

Keep learning and experiencing: You are free to earn and experience new jobs, projects or chores every day. Use this freedom to even learn a new skill from a local student or a local talent and work towards your passion.

Give: Share

One of Worksity’s benefits are allowing local businesses and local people to benefit from on-demand and no contract services carried out by local talents. Simply delegate your task, chores or projects to a student or a local talent with up-to-date knowledge of the industry, for a more effective and efficient outcome.

Type of projects you can delegate

SKILLED SERVICES

Skilled services are those where you require an applicant, with experience and training in a specific field. (Applicants with specific qualification or training). For example, Photography, Website Design, Logo Design or others.

MAINTENANCE / AD-HOC:

Maintenance services are those where you require an applicant with no formal qualification as these services are related to your everyday chores. For instance, Gardening, Pet Walking, Car-Wash or others.

DELIVERY

Delivery services are those where you require an applicant with no formal qualification, as these services are here to help you move items from A-B. For example: deliver groceries from your local supermarket to your doorsteps.

Safety & Security

Our escrow payment system ensures that your payments are only released on your approval and satisfaction of the project. All transactions are secured with SSL encryption. (Through Stripe)
